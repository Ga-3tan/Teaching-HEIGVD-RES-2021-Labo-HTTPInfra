Easy Japan is an app to help you study Japanese with flashcards, quizzes and more. Use this app to help memorize the Hiragana and Katakana with some clever trick and check if you remember them well with a quiz! You can create your own profile to gather your data online and earn some cool badges !

Interface

Navigate through Easy Japan easily with this simple and design interface, made for simplicity

You can install the app on Android to learn on the go, or just through your browser with the online app available here

And with an account, you can import your informations everywhere!

Tips and tricks!

Learn hiragana and katakana easily with the tips and tricks from a Japanese teacher!

For every character there's a different tips that will help you memorize your alphabet.

Test yourself!

You can test your knowledge and check your flaws by taking randomly generated quizzes, just choose your subject and difficulty and try to make 100% correct to progress and win some cool achievements!
